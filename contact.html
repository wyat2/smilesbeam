Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary initial consultations where we'll evaluate your smile, discuss treatment options, and answer all your questions.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers and will help you maximize your benefits. We also offer flexible payment plans.

What age should my child first see an orthodontist?

The American Association of Orthodontists recommends children have their first orthodontic evaluation by age 7.

How long does treatment typically take?

Treatment time varies based on individual needs, but most cases are completed in 12-24 months.

Do you offer Invisalign?

Yes! We're certified Invisalign providers and offer clear aligner treatment for both teens and adults.

What are your office hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, with Saturday appointments available by request.

Orthodontic Emergency?

If you're experiencing a true orthodontic emergency outside of office hours, please call our emergency line at (760) 555-9999. For non-urgent issues, you can use the contact form above or call during regular business hours.

Meet Our Team

Experienced professionals dedicated to your smile

Your Smile is in Expert Hands

Our team of experienced orthodontists and dedicated staff members are committed to providing you with the highest quality care in a warm, welcoming environment. We combine clinical excellence with genuine compassion to ensure every patient has a positive experience.

Dr. Rachel Beam

Lead Orthodontist & Practice Owner

Dr. Beam received her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from UCLA School of Dentistry and completed her orthodontic residency at UCSF. With over 15 years of experience, she is passionate about creating beautiful, healthy smiles and building lasting relationships with her patients.

Education:

  • DDS, UCLA School of Dentistry
  • Orthodontic Residency, UCSF
  • Board Certified, American Board of Orthodontics

Associate Orthodontist

  • Invisalign Certified Provider

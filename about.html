About Beam Orthodontics

Welcome to Our Practice

At Beam Orthodontics, we believe that everyone deserves a smile they can be proud of. Our practice combines cutting-edge orthodontic technology with personalized care to deliver exceptional results in a comfortable, welcoming environment.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality orthodontic care while creating a positive, enjoyable experience for our patients. We are committed to transforming smiles and improving lives through excellence in orthodontics.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted orthodontic practice in Encinitas and surrounding communities, known for our clinical excellence, innovative approach, and compassionate care.

Our Values

  • Excellence in everything we do
  • Compassion and respect for all patients
  • Integrity in our practice and relationships
  • Innovation through continuous learning
  • Community involvement and service

Our Practice

Beam Orthodontics opened its doors in Encinitas in 2020 with a simple goal: to provide exceptional orthodontic care in a warm, friendly environment. Since then, we've had the privilege of transforming thousands of smiles and building lasting relationships with our patients and their families.

Our state-of-the-art facility features the latest in orthodontic technology, comfortable treatment areas, and a welcoming atmosphere that puts patients at ease from the moment they walk through our doors.

Community Involvement

We're proud to be part of the Encinitas community and actively participate in local events, schools, and charitable initiatives. We believe in giving back to the community that has welcomed us so warmly.

From sponsoring local youth sports teams to participating in community health fairs, we're committed to making Encinitas an even better place to live, work, and smile.
